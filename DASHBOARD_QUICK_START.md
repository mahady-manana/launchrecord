# SIO V5 Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## What Changed?

The dashboard has been **completely redesigned** to help users understand their SIO-V5 audit results and take action.

## Key Features

### 1. Every Score is Explainable
- **Click any score** → See detailed breakdown of what it means, how it's calculated, and why it matters
- Scores now show grade (A+ to F) for easy interpretation
- Color-coded: Green (90-100), Lime (70-89), Orange (40-69), Red (0-39)

### 2. Every Action Has Guidance
- **Click any action** → See what to do, why it's important, how to implement, and an example
- Priority levels clearly marked (Critical/High/Medium)
- Implementation tips included

### 3. Dedicated Guidance Pages
Each pillar has a full guide explaining:
- What it is (in plain language)
- Why it matters (business impact)
- How to analyze your results
- How to improve (actionable steps)
- Marketing terms glossary

**Access guidance from:**
- "Understanding Your Report" link at the top
- "Learn about SIO-V5" button in pillars section
- Direct URLs: `/dashboard/[productId]/guidance/[pillar]`

## User Journey

### First Time Users
1. **Run Audit** → Get complete SIO-V5 analysis
2. **Read Overall Assessment** → Understand composite score and category position
3. **Review Ego Stab** → Get honest feedback on positioning
4. **Explore Pillars** → Click each pillar card for details
5. **Click Actions** → Learn how to implement each recommendation
6. **Visit Guidance** → Deep-dive into concepts you're unfamiliar with

### Returning Users
1. **Check Score Changes** → Compare to previous audits
2. **Review New Actions** → Focus on highest priority items
3. **Track Progress** → Watch scores improve over time

## Understanding the Sections

### Overall Assessment
- **Composite Score**: Your overall SIO optimization level (0-100)
- **Category Position**: How you're perceived (Leader/Challenger/Replicable/Invisible)
- **Primary Constraint**: Your biggest limiting factor - focus here first!
- **Survival Probability**: Likelihood of sustained growth (12 months)

### The Ego Stab
- **Brutal Summary**: Honest one-liner about your positioning
- **Severity**: How critical your issues are (1-100)
- **Cliché Density**: How much generic language you use (0-100%)
- **Founder Bias Risk**: How much you might be overestimating clarity

### Five Pillars
Each pillar card shows:
- **Score** (click for breakdown)
- **Performance Level** (e.g., "Instant", "Clear", "Confusing")
- **What/Why/How** sections explaining the metric
- **Priority Actions** (click each for implementation guidance)

## Common Questions

### "What does this score mean?"
→ Click the score to see detailed explanation

### "How do I improve this?"
→ Click any action item for step-by-step guidance

### "I don't understand this marketing term"
→ Visit the guidance pages or check the glossary in `/guidance/overview`

### "Which pillar should I focus on first?"
→ Start with your **Primary Constraint** shown in Overall Assessment

### "How often should I run audits?"
→ Recommended: Every 2-4 weeks, or after making significant changes

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start with the Primary Constraint** - highest leverage
2. **Read the What/Why/How** on each pillar card
3. **Click actions** to understand implementation
4. **Visit guidance pages** for concepts you're unfamiliar with
5. **Implement incrementally** - don't try to fix everything at once
6. **Re-audit after changes** to measure progress
7. **Share with your team** - everyone should understand the metrics
