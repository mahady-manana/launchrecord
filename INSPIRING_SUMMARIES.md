## Copywriting Principles

### 1. Aspirational Labels
Instead of "Excellent" → "Industry Leader"
Instead of "Critical" → "Untapped Potential"
Instead of "Weak" → "Story Beginning"

### 2. Belief-Driven Language
- "You're building something great"
- "Your product deserves attention"
- "You have what it takes to lead"
- "This is your origin story"

### 3. Opportunity Framing
Low scores aren't failures—they're:
- "Next growth chapter"
- "Breakthrough moment"
- "First chapter"
- "Untapped potential"

### 4. Actionable Inspiration
Every insight:
- Acknowledges current state
- Paints the possible future
- Provides first step
- Implies belief in founder

### 5. Emotional Resonance
- "This is your time"
- "Legends begin here"
- "Empires of trust"
- "Redefine the game"
