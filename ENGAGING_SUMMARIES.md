# Engaging Grade Summaries - Founder FOMO Edition

## Philosophy
**Don't just inform—inspire action.** Every grade summary should make founders feel something and want to improve.

## Structure
Each grade now has:
1. **Summary** - What the score means (emotional hook)
2. **Insight** - Actionable FOMO/urgency (💡 icon)

## Copywriting Principles Used

### 1. Emotional Hooks
- "Silent growth killer"
- "Conversion emergency"
- "Radio silence"
- "Competitors are winning"

### 2. FOMO Triggers
- "Leaving traffic on the table"
- "Not yet the default answer"
- "Invisible growth doesn't attract"
- "Before competitors notice"

### 3. Action-Oriented Insights
- Specific next steps
- Quantified potential ("10-20%")
- Time urgency ("Every day", "Start today")
- Clear cause-effect

### 4. Founder Psychology
- Appeals to competitive nature
- Highlights hidden problems
- Offers achievable fixes
- Celebrates wins without complacency
